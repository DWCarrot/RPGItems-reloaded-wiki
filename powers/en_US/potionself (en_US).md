## Properties

* duration

  * Type: int
  * **Required**

  Time of potion effect, in ticks

* cost

  * Type: int
  * Default: 0

  Durability cost of the power.

* cooldown

  * Type: long
  * **Required**

  Cooldown of the power, in ticks.

* amplifier

  * Type: int
  * **Required**

  Amplifier of potion effect

* type

  * Type: PotionEffectType
  * Default: HEAL

  Type of potion effect
